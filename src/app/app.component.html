Bia Ribeiro

Bia Ribeiro is a journalist who has graduated from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ).

She currently works as product and audience coordinator at Rádio Novelo, a Brazilian journalistic podcast production company. Her responsibilities are digital strategy, which includes systematizing the digital presence of the company's podcasts, communication with different audio platforms; distribution; audience reports; planning and preparing content for promotional channels, and social media metrics analyses.

Born in Rio de Janeiro in May 1996.
